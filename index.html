About Me

I'm a English Major Student

I am a student in the English Education Program, embarking on my academic journey to delve deeper into the science of education and language teaching. Through this study, I hope to develop a profound understanding of various methods and strategies for teaching English, both theoretically and practically. Additionally, I aim to build communication skills, critical thinking, and creativity, which will support my role as a future educator. For me, this program is not only about learning the language but also about using it as a tool to help others achieve better opportunities in the global world. I believe that education is a long-term investment, and I am ready to contribute to creating a positive impact on others' lives through what I am learning today.
I chose the English Education Program because of my strong interest in language and communication, especially English as an international language. I believe that proficiency in English not only opens up many career opportunities but also serves as a key to understanding global cultures. Furthermore, I aspire to contribute to the field of education by helping others master English so that they can access broader opportunities in education, employment, and daily life.
In the future, I hope to become an inspiring and competent English educator. I aim to implement innovative and effective teaching methods so that my students not only understand English but also feel confident in using it. Additionally, I aspire to continuously improve myself through experiences and learning, allowing me to make a meaningful and tangible impact in the field of education, both locally and globally.
